[Diagram: Physical AI - Comprehensive Architecture for Autonomous Humanoid Intelligence]

This diagram presents the comprehensive architecture for autonomous humanoid intelligence in Physical AI systems. The visualization encompasses:

1. **Multimodal Perception System**:
   - Advanced computer vision with real-time scene understanding
   - Multi-sensor fusion (LiDAR, cameras, IMU, force/torque sensors)
   - 3D environment mapping and dynamic scene analysis
   - Attention-based resource allocation for efficient processing
   - Robust perception under uncertainty and environmental variations

2. **Cognitive Intelligence Core**:
   - Memory-augmented neural architectures for reasoning
   - Multi-modal knowledge representation and retrieval
   - Probabilistic inference and decision-making under uncertainty
   - Lifelong learning and knowledge transfer mechanisms
   - Context-aware planning with temporal and spatial reasoning

3. **Embodied Action Control**:
   - Whole-body motion planning and control for dexterous manipulation
   - Bipedal locomotion with dynamic balance and stability
   - Navigation in complex, human-populated environments
   - Socially-aware behavior generation and expression
   - Multi-task coordination and resource allocation

4. **System Integration Framework**:
   - Real-time distributed computing and communication
   - ROS 2-based component orchestration and message passing
   - Computational resource management and optimization
   - Human-robot interaction interface design
   - System health monitoring and maintenance

5. **Safety and Reliability**:
   - Formal verification of safety-critical behaviors
   - Real-time constraint checking and validation
   - Fail-safe mechanisms and graceful degradation
   - Human oversight and intervention protocols
   - Continuous safety monitoring and risk assessment

6. **Adaptive Learning and Interaction**:
   - Human-in-the-loop learning and feedback integration
   - Social intelligence for natural human-robot collaboration
   - Personalization and adaptation to individual users
   - Continuous skill acquisition and improvement
   - Trust-building through reliable and predictable behavior

The diagram demonstrates the complete integration of all components into a sophisticated autonomous humanoid system capable of intelligent, safe, and natural interaction in complex real-world environments.
