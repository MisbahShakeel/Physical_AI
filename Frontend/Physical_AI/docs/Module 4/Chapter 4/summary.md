## Key Takeaways

- **Autonomous humanoid architecture** integrates perception, cognition, and action systems into a unified framework
- **System integration principles** emphasize modularity, real-time processing, and fault tolerance
- **Multimodal perception** combines visual, auditory, and sensor inputs for comprehensive awareness
- **Cognitive architecture** implements memory, reasoning, and planning systems
- **Action coordination** manages navigation, manipulation, and interaction behaviors
- **Safety mechanisms** provide validation and emergency response protocols
- **Performance evaluation** measures success across multiple dimensions
- **Scalable design** enables future enhancements and research
